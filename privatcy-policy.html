Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data from you:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Demographic information: Age, gender, and zip code
  • Usage information: Information about your use of the Website, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the time you spend on the Website.
  • Device information: Information about the device you use to access the Website, such as the type of device, browser, operating system, and IP address.
We collect data through various ways, including:
  • Forms: When you contact us through forms on the Website, you may be asked to provide your contact information and other relevant data.
  • Surveys: We may conduct surveys from time to time, and you may voluntarily choose to participate and provide your data.
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your usage of the Website. Cookies are small files placed on your device that store information about your preferences and activity. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.
How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Website and its services.
  • To identify your preferred real estate options and send you relevant listings.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide you with customer support.
  • To improve the Website and its services.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
Sharing Your Data

We do not share your personal data with any third-party vendors or partners without your consent. However, we may disclose your personal data if required by law or in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with a court order or other legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Homeland or others.
  • To investigate or prevent fraud or other illegal activity.
Your Data Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to access and review your personal data.
  • Correction: You have the right to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request that your personal data be deleted.
  • Restriction: You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal data.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data.
Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use two-factor authentication (2FA) for added security.

Opt-Out Mechanisms

You may opt out of the collection of your personal data by using the opt-out mechanisms provided on the Website or by contacting us through the contact information provided below.

About Divas Agency

Diva's Agency is a platform that helps users find affordable housing options with reasonable instalment payment plans. This platform aims to increase accessibility to home ownership, particularly for individuals and families facing financial constraints by introducing affordable housing options, flexible and sustainable payment structures, as well as a platform where those looking to sell properties can interact with buyers.
